Lazer Lab Reading Group

Logistics

The reading group meets weekly (currently: Fridays at 2pm) to discuss a paper or two. The discussion is largely unstructured; if someone has recommended a paper they may be asked to introduce it, but there is no designated discussion leader.

Email me (mccabe.s at northeastern dot edu) if:

  • you would like a calendar invite with the Zoom link
  • you would like to suggest a paper to read
  • you are having trouble accessing one of the papers
