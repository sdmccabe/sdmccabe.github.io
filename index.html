Stefan D. McCabe

I am a doctoral student at Northeastern University’s Network Science Institute under David Lazer. My research focuses on the interplay between personal social networks, social media, and political behavior. Before joining Northeastern I was a graduate student at George Mason University’s Department of Computational Social Science, where I completed a master’s thesis under Rob Axtell on agent-based modeling methodologies. In Summer 2020 I was a research intern at Microsoft Research, working with David Rothschild.
